> Human-only file. Agents must not edit this.

## Product

Mobile-first web platform for Czech primary school parent committees.

## Business goal

Simplify financial contributions, volunteer task coordination, and feedback collection for one real school parent committee. Prove the model before scaling.

## Primary users

- Parents (self-registered, Czech-speaking primarily)
- Parent committee representatives
- Teachers and school staff

## Success criteria for MVP

- One school successfully using the platform
- Parents can generate and pay via QR bank transfer
- Committee can confirm payments manually
- At least one volunteer task cycle completed
- Public transparency page is live

## SLA (target)

- Uptime: 99% during school term hours
- Payment QR generation: < 2s
- Page load on mobile 4G: < 3s
- Voice message transcribed and saved: 3s

## Legal context

- Funds owned by registered `zapsaný spolek` (Czech civil association)
- No personal bank account for committee funds
- GDPR applies (EU/Czech law)
- No school data import without parent consent

## Constraints

- No child accounts in MVP
- No public individual payment visibility
- No school-provided parent import
